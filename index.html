About the Club

We are a technical community focused on learning by building. Members work across disciplines to design systems that interact with the physical world and the internet. Through hands-on projects, workshops, and collaborative development, we explore the full stack of IoT—from circuit design to cloud infrastructure. Whether you're interested in robotics, smart home technology, or industrial automation, there's a place for you here.
